The Grand Teton mountains

This mountain range is located in western Wyoming in Teton Nation park.


Teton national Park is aproximately 310,000 acres of pristine mountains, forests, and prairies. The Teton mountain range is also the youngest mountains included in the Rocky mountain range. These mountains lie on a fault line which caused the dramatic differences in elevation between the valley and the mountain tops towering above.

Table of landmarks

The Grand The highest peak in the Teton mountain range
Jenny Lake A beautiful glacier formed lake at the base of the mountains. With amazing hiking trails
Jackson Lake The largest lake in teton nation park.
